Product Description

ExtremeHunter is a brand under Valley Sports, which specializes in designing and manufacturing high quality sporting goods including tactical hunting parka. We target to provide highly breathable fabrics to offer more comfort to release the excessive body heat and moisture.

Two extra pockets in axilla allows people to control the size of the pocket to release heat yet retain certain level to keep warm. The parka will be suitable for heavy duty outdoor activity and field work from late fall to early spring in cold weather. The large tool pocket gives more room for extra storage or a foam pad to support our body to lean on trees. The keyring design will help us keep our car key or remote in a safer place to prevent loosing car key in a remote area. Windproof, waterproof and sound control makes your every movement quiet. Anti-scratching fabric offers highly breathable membrane to breath better than other products. Talon Waterproof zipper gives you a better choice for tougher environment. The fabric is tested falling in the range of 11,000-15,000 mm waterproof rating.
